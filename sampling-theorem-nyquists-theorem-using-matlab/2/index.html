Conclusion

We can see from the plots that the frequency of the Under-Sampled signal is not exactly same as that of the actual analog signal. When the frequency of the signal to be sampled was given as 200Hz, the frequency of the Under-Sampled signal was found to be 40Hz. This condition is also known as aliasing.
But the signal frequency of the Perfectly Sampled and the Over-Sampled signal is exactly same as that of the analog signal.
